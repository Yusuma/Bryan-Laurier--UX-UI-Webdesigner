LCHS SUD 41

The association Luttons Contre le Harcèlement Scolaire SUD 41 is an organization fighting against bullying in schools as well as on social media. In order to communicate its actions and inform its volunteers, the association wanted a complete redesign of its website and enlisted my services. The website features an overview of the association, event calendar functionalities, the ability to reserve spots for participation in events, and a dedicated members' area to access documentation and information about the actions and causes supported by the association.
